Peak Hours Analysis

Rush hours (7-9 AM, 5-7 PM) account for 40% of daily trips. Average trip duration increases by 25% during these periods due to traffic congestion. Weekend patterns show different peaks at 11 AM and 9 PM.

Payment Trends

Credit card usage has increased to 78% of all transactions. Trips paid by card show 15% higher tip percentages on average. Mobile payments growing at 23% month-over-month.

Distance Patterns
